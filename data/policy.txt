# Remote Work Policy
Employees are allowed to work remotely up to 3 days a week. Mandatory office days are Tuesday and Thursday. 

# Hardware Budget
Every new employee receives a $2,000 stipend for home office equipment. This must be expensed within the first 30 days.

# Vacation Days
Standard employees receive 20 days of Paid Time Off (PTO) per year. Unused PTO does not roll over to the next calendar year.
